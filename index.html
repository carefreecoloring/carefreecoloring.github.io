About Carefree Coloring

Books that feel cheerful, easy to give, and fun to open.

Carefree Coloring started with a simple idea: make creative books that look as playful as they feel. The covers are bold, the themes are approachable, and the pages are meant to invite a little pause in a busy day.

Whether it's a vehicle book for a toddler, a mandala collection for a quiet evening, or a Miniature Schnauzer journal for everyday notes, we keep the mood light and the artwork front and center.
