Privacy Policy

Privacy Notice

How Carefree Coloring handles information collected through our website and related services.

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Carefree Coloring ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website, interact with our content, or contact us. This policy applies to our website and any related services or applications that link to it.

Information We Collect

We may collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: If you contact us by email or through another communication channel, we may receive your name, email address, and the contents of your message.
  • Information collected automatically: When you visit our website, we or our service providers may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, referring pages, pages viewed, and approximate location derived from your IP address.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We may use cookies and similar technologies to support analytics, understand site traffic, and improve user experience.
  • Third-party interactions: If you click a link to a third-party site, such as Amazon or a social media platform, that third party may collect information about your visit in accordance with its own privacy policy.

How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and content.
  • Respond to questions, requests, and customer support inquiries.
  • Analyze traffic and usage trends to understand how visitors use our site.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our website and services.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies where necessary.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: With vendors that help us operate the website, such as hosting providers, analytics providers, and email services.
  • Legal requirements: If we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • Business protection: To protect our rights, users, property, or safety, or to investigate fraud or abuse.
  • Third-party sites: When you choose to visit a third-party site through links on our website, your information will be handled under that third party's own privacy policy.

Analytics and Advertising

We currently use Google Analytics to better understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your interactions with the site. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use its services by reviewing Google's privacy information.

We may also include affiliate links, including links to Amazon. If you click those links, Amazon or other third parties may track the referral and process related information under their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the website. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, but that may limit your ability to contact us or use some site features. You can also control cookies through your browser settings, although disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at hello@carefreecoloring.com.
