Privacy Policy

How Carefree Coloring handles information collected through our website and related services.

This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit our site or contact us.

Effective date

May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Carefree Coloring ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website, interact with our content, or contact us.

Information we collect

What we may receive

We may collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically through website traffic and device signals, and information from cookies or similar technologies.

  • If you email us, we may receive your name, email address, and message content.
  • We may collect IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, referring pages, and pages viewed.
  • Third-party sites you click through to may collect information under their own policies.
How we use information

Why we use it

We use information to operate and improve the site, respond to questions, analyze traffic, protect the site, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

Limited disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, when required by law, or to protect our rights and users.

Analytics and advertising

Tracking and affiliate links

We currently use Google Analytics to understand site usage. We may also include affiliate links, including Amazon links, which may be tracked by the third party.

Children's privacy

Who this site is for

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the website.

Contact

Questions about privacy

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, please contact us at hello@carefreecoloring.com.
